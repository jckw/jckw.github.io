I’m Jack, and at the moment I’m a Computer Science and Philosophy student at Oxford. I love reading about startups, messing around trying to design things, and eating pizza amongst an array of similarly nutritious foods.

I’m also into making and half-baking web apps. Almost every web app I’ve built has used Django for the backend. One or two used Django templates for the frontend, but more recently I’ve been using React because I’m a cool kid and modern. I’ve dabbled with React Native whilst interning at Finimize too, and quite like it.

Web apps are a lot of fun, but who knows whether I’ll be making them forever.

Looking to the future, I’m thinking about doing something in Cognitive Science or Data Engineering/Science, as vague as that sounds.

In the meantime, I’m looking forward to taking more courses in artificial intelligence, machine learning, algorithms, and logic, as well as course in Knowledge and Philosophy of Mind. Hopefully I’ll be building and half-building some more things along the way.

Feel free to send me an email/message/tweet/whatever if you have any questions about jobs, computer science, philosophy, or Oxford.
