until.dev

2024 · Open Source Funding

until is a new funding model for open-source software that makes it possible for small and medium-sized projects to receive ad hoc funding in the form of reverse-bounties.

Library users can create bounties to incentivise the development of features, bug fixes, and documentation. When users donate to a bounty, they can set an expiry date after which they will automatically receive a refund.
